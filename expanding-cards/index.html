Starter Anime for Beginners

One Punch Man

2015

It tells the story of Saitama, a superhero who, because he can defeat any opponent with a single punch, grows bored from a lack of challenge.

My Hero Academia

2016

Set in a world where superpowers (called "Quirks") have become commonplace, the story follows a boy who was born without a Quirk but still dreams of becoming a superhero.

Spy X Family

2022

The story follows a spy who has to "build a family" to execute a mission.

Demon Slayer

2019

It follows teenage Tanjiro Kamado, who strives to become a Demon Slayer after his family was slaughtered.

Death Note

2006

The story follows Light Yagami, a genius high school student who discovers a mysterious notebook: the "Death Note" that grants the user the ability to kill anyone whose name is written in its pages.
